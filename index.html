Crop Circle Farms

Accelerated, controlled, protected and sustainable agriculture.

  • Sustainable Agriculture

    Sustainable agriculture integrates environmental health with economic viability. Crop Circle farming promotes soil health, minimizes water use and fosters a food safe growing environment for produce.

  • Controlled Agriculture

    Controlled agriculture uses technology to reduce the use of soil, water and fertilizers without sacrificing production. A Crop Circle Farm reduces operating costs by 60% compared to open field farming methods.

  • Protected Agriculture

    Protected agriculture virtually eliminates the potential for damage caused by pests or disease, increases overall efficiency, maximized production and saves valuable resources including soil and water.

  • Accelerated Agriculture

    Accelerated agriculture systems grow more food in a shorter period of time. Crop Circle Farm systems cycle crops every 70 days on average, which maximizes the growing potential of a short season farm.
